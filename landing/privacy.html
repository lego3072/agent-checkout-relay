What We Collect

  • Lead form details you submit (email, company, use case, optional website).
  • Operational metadata required for abuse prevention and onboarding workflows.
  • Billing events from Stripe needed to activate and maintain account access.

Payments

Payments are processed by Stripe. Card details are handled by Stripe, not stored by Agent Checkout Relay.

How We Use Data

  • Route qualified buyers to the selected checkout path.
  • Issue and rotate API keys for active paid accounts.
  • Maintain account status for renewals, failed payments, and access control.
